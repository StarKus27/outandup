Activities.

Here at Out and Up we provide anyone and everyone with activites right for you!

We offer many great activites here at Out and Up, six in total, ranging from things such as Archery to even Water rafting.

Here at Out and Up we offer great activities, and one of our most popular is archery and our Assault course. Whether you like to stay indoors or outdoors, Out and Up has the right activity for you. So why not try out something like Tennis, where with this great activity you are given the oppurtunity to play either inside or outside. This is perfect for anyone, as you can choose to be in the warmth of the indoor court or feel the nice breeze of the outdoors.

If you don't like Tennis then there is an also Assault course or even rock climbing here at Out and Up, so ask yourself one question, do you want an adrenaline rush and do you want an extreme feeling of blood pumping through your body? As you climb over tall walls and crawl under nets and jump into mud. So if you want to expierience a rush through your body then choosing to take part in either of these two actvities is the right thing for you to do. So get ready to have a great time at Out and Up because you will not be disappointed!

However most people may not enjoy going through such an adrenaline rush, so why not go to our swimming pool. why not have a nice and calm swim either by yourself or with friends to sooth your mind and to just relax from the outside world. Swimming is a great exercise as well as a relaxation method as you can either race your friends or just chill out by the side as you fall into a deep relaxation. Life guards are always on patrol at the swimming pool, they are trained and ready for any situations which could possibly happen. There are an estimate of 2 lifeguards at least on duty, so feel free to ask about any concerns that you may have. Our indoor swimming pool facility is located close to the reception and lodges so feel free to come by anytime.

If swimming is something you like and are looking for some more extreme water related activity then why not try water rafting! Water rafting is a great team building exercise which involves the whole teams help to move forward and achieve their goal. You will be fitted with full gear and equipment that is need to provide you with the best experience. Once you have your necessary required equipment, you will be given a guide to help teach you the basics. Every participant will endure in a series of drills to help everybody understand how to safely get yourself through this thrilling activity. Also, a guide will be with you along the whole way of your adventure through the water rapids, in case of any emergencies which may occur, however it is very unlikely. So get ready for a great adventure riding the rapids on the splashing current and remember to hold on, as safety is our number one priority!
